ARt

This was a presentation made for Peter's assessment 2. The brief was to develop a creative technology project proposal and prototype where possible, then give a presentation on it.
We (Gaia, James, Masha, Michael and I) thought of making an AR drawing app, and thought up of ways to monetize it so it can be a successful business idea. We named our project ARt, as a combination of AR and art.

Presentation Screenshots

These are some screenshots from the presentation. I worked on the idea generation, research on similar existing ideas, sketching, designing, and protoyping with Unity.
This was the first time I made a project in AR using the phone. I found the setting up a little difficult and it took a while, but I managed to get it done in the end. The Unity project had simple UI in the end, with the ability to recognise an image (in this case the Unity logo), then position AR objects in the world using the Unity logo as reference.

Additional comments

I think we did alright with this idea, research, and presentation. The prototype could have been more advanced if we had more time to work on it, but I did learn a little about working on AR in Unity using AR kit.
