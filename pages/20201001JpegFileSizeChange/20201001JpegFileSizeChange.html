Jpeg File Size Change

This was a class taught by Carl where students experimented with jpeg compression.
I used Affinity Photo to change between levels in quality.

Original Image

Picture I took at a beach in Singapore.

JPG at 10 quality

The loss in color quality can be noticed, especially in the clouds.

JPG at 4 quality

Some color has been lost and turned into grayscale, and there are only several shades of colors left.

JPG at 0 quality

Similar to the image at 4 quality, most colors are gone, a few shades are left.
Even more of the image is in grayscal compared to the image at 4 quality.

Additional comments

I also noticed that when I zoomed in, details in the images are lost.
This was harder to notice as the initial image was large, but would be more noticable with smaller images.
